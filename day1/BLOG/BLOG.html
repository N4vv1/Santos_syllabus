Dishes

Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish made with vinegared rice and various ingredients such as raw fish, seafood, vegetables, and seaweed (nori). It is commonly served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger. Popular types of sushi include nigiri, maki rolls, and sashimi.

Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a flat bread dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, vegetables, and meats. It is baked in a hot oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted. Pizza is enjoyed around the world and has many different styles and flavors.

Hamburger

A hamburger is a popular American fast food made with a grilled beef patty inside a sliced bun. It is usually topped with lettuce, tomato, cheese, onions, pickles, and sauces like ketchup and mayonnaise.
