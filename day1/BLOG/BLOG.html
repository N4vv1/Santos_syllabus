Blueberry Pie

A pie is a baked dish typically made of a pastry dough casing filled with sweet or savory ingredients. It can be served hot or cold and varies widely in size, crust type, and filling across cultures.

Takoyaki

Takoyaki is a popular Japanese street food originating from Osaka in the 1930s, invented by street vendor Tomekichi Endo. Often called "octopus balls," they are made from a wheat flour batter mixed with dashi (Japanese fish stock), filled with diced octopus, tempura scraps (tenkasu), pickled red ginger (benishoga), and green onions.

Pizza

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin, consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese (typically mozzarella), olive oil, oregano, and various other ingredients like vegetables, meats, and herbs. It is baked quickly in a hot oven—often wood-fired—and served hot.
